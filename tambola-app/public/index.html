TAMBOLA RULES

  • Each player gets a unique ticket with 15 numbers.
  • The game calls numbers from 1 to 90 one by one.
  • If a called number is on your ticket, you mark it.
  • The first player to mark all numbers in a winning pattern claims a prize.

Winning Combinations:

  • Jaldi 5: First 5 numbers marked on your ticket.
  • Lines: Completing all numbers in the Top, Middle, or Bottom row.
  • Full House: All 15 numbers on your ticket are marked. This is the biggest prize!
